Researchers, engineers, and students at WAVESLAB.

Giulia Cisotto, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor / Lab Director

Department of Mathematics, Informatics, and Geosciences

University of Trieste, Italy

Giulia Cisotto is a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor (RTT) at the Department of Mathematics, Informatics and Geosciences at the University of Trieste, Italy.

Since November 1st, 2025, she heads the Waves Lab at the University of Trieste, focusing on AI and machine learning modeling of biological time-series with applications to healthcare, Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI), and computational neuroscience.

She earned her Italian National Scientific Habilitation for Associate Professor in Computer Science (2025) and Telecommunication Engineering (2024). Previously, she served as a non-tenure Assistant Professor at the University of Milano-Bicocca (2022–2024) and the University of Padova (2019–2022). She was also a Post-doctoral Researcher in Padova and a Research Associate at Keio University of Tokyo, Japan.

Shayan Sharifi

Ph.D. Researcher

WAVESLAB Member

University of Trieste, Italy

Shayan Sharifi is a PhD candidate in AI and Applied Data Science, specializing in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision.

His research primarily focuses on Explainable Multi-Modal Learning and the development of machine/deep learning models and signal processing pipelines for physiological time-series data, particularly ECG and EEG signals.

He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering and a Master’s degree in ICT Engineering from the University of Padova. Alongside his academic research, he brings a strong industry background as an AI Engineer, Web Developer, and former Head of IT.
