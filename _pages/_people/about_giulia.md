**Giulia Cisotto** is a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor (RTT) at the Department of Mathematics, Informatics and Geosciences at the **University of Trieste, Italy**. 

Since November 1st, 2025, she heads the **Waves Lab** at the University of Trieste, focusing on AI and machine learning modeling of biological time-series with applications to healthcare, Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI), and computational neuroscience.

She earned her Italian National Scientific Habilitation for Associate Professor in Computer Science (2025) and Telecommunication Engineering (2024). Previously, she served as a non-tenure Assistant Professor at the [University of Milano-Bicocca](https://www.unimib.it/) (2022–2024) and the [University of Padova](https://www.unipd.it/) (2019–2022). She was also a Post-doctoral Researcher in Padova and a Research Associate at [Keio University of Tokyo](https://www.keio.ac.jp/en/), Japan.
