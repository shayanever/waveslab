**Shayan Sharifi** is a PhD candidate in AI and Applied Data Science, specializing in **Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision**.

His research primarily focuses on **Explainable Multi-Modal Learning** and the development of machine/deep learning models and signal processing pipelines for physiological time-series data, particularly **ECG and EEG signals**. 

He holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and a Master's degree in ICT Engineering from the [University of Padova](https://www.unipd.it/en/). Alongside his academic research, he brings a strong industry background as an **AI Engineer**, **Web Developer**, and former **Head of IT**.
